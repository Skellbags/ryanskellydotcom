Info

Dark is a german written Netflix series, in the Sci-Fi genre. It is about a kid named Jonas, whose father commited suicide. Jonas had a funny feeling about his town of Winden. He soon finds out that there was a time traveling cult that was pulling strings around the town. Jonas' friend Mickel gets kidnapped by the cult and he becomes trapped in 1986. When Jonas goes to try to save him, he finds out that Mickel grows up to become his dad. This is shocking to Jonas because that means that his love intrest, Martha(Mickel's Sister), is actually his aunt. Jonas finds out that he can't change time, but hopes that by closing the worm hole that the time travelers use he can end his own existance.

What else happens?

Jonas finds out in season 2 that he grows up to become the leader of the time traveling cult, so by ending his own existance he can reverse time. So he tries to close it, but it ends up sending him to the future, and creatinng the worm hole he was trying to destroy. This is frustrating to Jonas, and in his rage, he goes to try to kill his older self. This has adverse affects because once he finds his older self, his older self kills Martha. Once this happens, the worm hole closes in on itself, destroying all of winden. Luckily, a version of Martha from another dimension comes to save Jonas, and takes him to her timeline
